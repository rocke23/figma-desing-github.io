About Us

We aim to build a vibrant and engaged community of people who can access the best of what aviation in Aotearoa New Zealand has to offer.

As a media source for the aviation industry in New Zealand, we connect student pilots with flight schools and aero clubs around the country to cater to your needs. Offering a comprehensive directory for everything

aviation, you will find all you need for becoming a pilot and pre-flight planning. We also offer support through our resources and products to assist flight schools in developing their student pilots.

Innovation is key to our new enterprise, and our resources combine the latest developments in aviation training with a proven, traditional approach.
